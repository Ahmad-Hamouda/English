⏱️ Realistic timeline: 6–9 months at 1 hour daily · ~2,500 new words to learn
📚Vocabulary15 min
Learn 10 new words from the Common Words tab. Say each word and its example sentence out loud 3 times. Write one new sentence of your own using each word.
🎧Listening15 min
Watch/listen to content slightly above your level. Great free options: BBC Learning English, EnglishClass101 (YouTube), easy podcasts. Use English subtitles, not Arabic.
🗣️Speaking (Shadowing)15 min
Pick 5 sentences. Play the audio, then repeat immediately copying the rhythm. Record yourself on your phone and compare. This is the fastest way to sound natural.
📖Reading + Grammar15 min
Read one short article (try News in Levels, level 2→3). Underline new words. Then study one grammar point from the Grammar Path tab.

🔑 Rules that actually work

  • Consistency beats intensity — 1 hour daily > 7 hours on Sunday
  • Speak out loud every day, even alone — fluency is a muscle
  • Learn words inside sentences, never as isolated lists
  • Review yesterday's 10 words before learning today's
  • Don't fear mistakes — they are how the brain learns
